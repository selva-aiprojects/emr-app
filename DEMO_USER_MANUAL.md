### Clinical Workflow

#### Patient Registration
1. Navigate to **Patients** section
2. Click **Register New Patient**
3. Enter patient demographics:
   - Personal information (name, age, gender)
   - Contact details
   - Emergency contact
   - Insurance information
   - Medical history
4. Click **Save**

#### Clinical Consultation (EMR)
1. Select patient from queue or search
2. Click **Start Consultation**
3. Complete clinical assessment:
   - **Chief Complaint:** Primary reason for visit
   - **History of Present Illness:** Detailed symptom description
   - **Past Medical History:** Chronic conditions, surgeries
   - **Physical Examination:** Vital signs and examination findings
   - **Assessment:** Diagnosis
   - **Plan:** Treatment recommendations

#### Prescription Management
1. In EMR interface, click **Add Prescription**
2. Enter medication details:
   - Medication name
   - Dosage (e.g., 500mg)
   - Frequency (e.g., Three times daily)
   - Duration (e.g., 7 days)
   - Special instructions
3. Click **Add to Prescription**
4. Review and **Save**

#### Lab Orders
1. Click **Order Lab Tests**
2. Select required investigations:
   - Blood tests (CBC, Lipid Profile, etc.)
   - Imaging (X-ray, CT, MRI)
   - Specialized tests
3. Add clinical indications
4. Click **Submit Order**

#### Patient History Review
- **Previous Encounters:** View all past consultations
- **Lab Results:** Review diagnostic reports
- **Medication History:** Track prescribed medications
- **Allergies:** Check for drug allergies and reactions

### Daily Workflow
1. **Morning Rounds:** Review inpatient list
2. **OPD Consultations:** See scheduled patients
3. **Emergency Cases:** Attend urgent cases
4. **Documentation:** Complete all EMR entries
5. **Referrals:** Manage specialist referrals
