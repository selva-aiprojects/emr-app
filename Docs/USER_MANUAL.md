## 🏢 2. HOSPITAL MANAGER GUIDE (Admin)
**Goal:** Setup your hospital details and manage your staff.

### Step 1: Hospital Name & Logo
1.  Go to **Hospital Settings** -> **Hospital Info & Logo**.
2.  Choose your **Hospital Theme Colors** to match your clinic's style.
3.  Upload your hospital **Logo** (PNG format); this logo will appear on all printed prescriptions and bills.

### Step 2: Staff & Bed Setup
1.  Go to **Hospital Departments** to list your specialties (like Heart, Kids, Bones).
2.  In **Bed Tracking**, add your wards and beds.
3.  Use **Staff Logins** to create accounts for your doctors, nurses, and office staff.

### Step 3: Daily Summary Check
*   Check the **Hospital Summary Dashboard** to see today's Total Income, New Patients, and Stock Levels.
*   Check **Activity Records** to see who made changes to any patient records or bills.

## 🩺 3. DOCTOR’S DAILY DESK
**Goal:** Easy patient check-ups and writing notes.

### Step 1: Find Patient Record
1.  Use the **Search Bar** at the top to find a patient by Name or ID Number.
2.  Check the **Patient Health Journey** in the sidebar to seen their past visits and test reports.

### Step 2: Patient Check-up (Visit)
1.  Click **Start Visit** (or New Assessment) to begin talking to the patient.
2.  Write down the **Patient's Problem**, your **Final Diagnosis**, and any **Advice**.
3.  **Vitals**: Type in BP, Pulse, and Temp. If the numbers are dangerous, they will turn Red immediately.

### Step 3: Medicines & Orders
1.  Add medicines using the **Medicines & Dose** (Rx) section. Choose Dose and how many Days.
2.  Click **Print Medicine Slip** to give a professional printed prescription to the patient.
3.  (Optional) Order **Labs & Tests**; these will show up in the Lab staff's list immediately.

---

## 👩‍⚕️ 4. NURSE & BED CARE WORKFLOW
**Goal:** Managing admitted patients and beds.

### Step 1: Admitting a Patient (Bed Stay)
1.  Find the patient and click **Admit Patient**.
2.  Choose a **Ward** and **Bed**. The bed will show as "Busy" for everyone else.
3.  Choose the doctor who is looking after this patient.

### Step 2: Daily Bed Checks
1.  Enter the patient's BP and Temp every few hours in the **Bed Tracking** area.
2.  Check if the patient has taken their medicines in the **Medicine Given Log**.

### Step 3: Discharge (Going Home)
1.  When the doctor says the patient can go home, check with the **Billing Desk** if the bill is paid.
2.  Once paid, click **Discharge** to free up the bed for a new patient.

---

## 💊 5. PHARMACY & MEDICINE STORE
**Goal:** Giving medicines and managing your store stock.

### Step 1: Medicines to Give
1.  Open the **Medicine & Store** page.
2.  You will see new prescriptions from doctors here. Click **Give Medicine** when you hand them over.
3.  The system will automatically reduce the count from your stock.

### Step 2: Store Management
1.  Check the **Stock Levels**. If something is low, it will show an Orange Warning.
2.  Use the **Add Stock** button when new medicines arrive from the supplier.

---

## 🔬 6. LAB & TEST REPORTS
**Goal:** Entering test results and printing reports.

### Step 1: Tests to Done
1.  New test orders from doctors show up in the **Lab & Test Reports** page.
2.  Click **Enter Result** for the test you finished.

### Step 2: Finish Report
1.  Type in the numbers (like Hemoglobin or Sugar).
2.  Click **Save & Finish**: The results go straight to the Doctor's screen and the Patient's file.

---

## 💰 7. BILLS & PAYMENTS (Cashier)
**Goal:** Making bills and taking money.

### Step 1: Make Bill
1.  Go to **Bills & Payments**.
2.  Click **Calculate Total Bill** to see a list of everything (Doctor fee + Medicine + Lab + Bed).
3.  Print the **Hospital Bill / Invoice** (PDF).

### Step 2: Take Payment
1.  Choose how the patient paid (Cash, Card, PhonePe/GPay, or Insurance).
2.  If it's Insurance, enter their Policy Number.
3.  **Mark as Paid**: This allows the patient to be discharged from their bed.

---

## 🚨 8. HELP & SYSTEM TIPS
*   **Active Sync**: Check for the blue pulsing light at the top; it means your data is being saved safely.
*   **Get Help**: If you see an error or a page doesn't work, go to **Facility Help** or **Settings** to tell the hospital manager.
*   **Security**: Always click **"Logout"** (or Exit Session) when you leave your desk.
